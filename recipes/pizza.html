Sicilian Pizza

Sicilian pizza is cooked in a square pan with plenty of olive oil. The important thing to remember is is to give the hdough enough time to rize. This results in a softer crust layer that is similar to Focaccia bread.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Kosher Salt
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Olive Oil

Directions:

  1. Make dough
  2. Coat with olive oil
  3. Ready the oven
  4. Put in oven
