Spicy Tuna Roll

Spicy Tuna Roll is one of those sushi staples in Japanese Restaurants across the US. Spicy tuna roll is made of 3 main ingredients. Sushi rice (steamed rice seasoned with sushi vinegar), nori (seaweed), and sashimi-grade tuna.

Ingredients:

  • Sushi Rice
  • Sashimi Tuna
  • Vinegar
  • Siracha Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Seaweed

Steps:

  1. Cook and roll rice
  2. Spread rice on nori(seaweed)
  3. Spread tuna, with siracha sauce
  4. Roll gently with roller
