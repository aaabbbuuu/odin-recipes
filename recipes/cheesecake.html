Cheesecake Recipe

NY Style Cheesecake

Though the ingredients are simple, cheesecake can be a little intimidating to make. Because cheesecake is a custard, it needs to be baked gently in a water bath, and you have to worry about the surface of the cake cracking, as well as over or undercooking.

Ingredients:

  • Graham Crackers
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Lemon Zest

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Make crust
  3. Make cheesecake batter
  4. Bake
